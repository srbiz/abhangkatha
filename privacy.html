Introduction

Welcome to the Abhang Katha Sangrah privacy policy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

We take data security seriously. All data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and security practices. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

You have the right to:

Our application is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
