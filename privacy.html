Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 13, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.
We prioritize your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.
Data Collection & Google Play Declaration

We collect minimal data required for app functionality and improvement. Specific data types collected are declared in our Google Play Data Safety section:

  • Device or other IDs (Required): Android Advertising ID and Firebase Installation ID for app functionality and Google AdMob ads.
  • Analytics Data (Optional): Firebase Analytics collects app usage statistics, performance data, and crash reports to improve the app.
  • No Location Data: We do NOT collect location information (GPS or network-based).
  • No Personal Information: We do NOT collect personally identifiable information like name, email, phone, or contacts.
How We Use Your Information

All collected data is used only for legitimate purposes outlined in Google Play Data Safety standards:

  • App Functionality: Essential features and content delivery using device identifiers.
  • Performance & Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics through Firebase Analytics to improve app performance.
  • Crash Reporting & Fixes: Error reports and app state data to identify and resolve technical issues.
  • Advertising (AdMob): Device identifiers and app interaction data for personalized advertising revenue.
Data Sharing & Third Parties

We have a strict no-sharing policy. However, limited anonymized data sharing occurs for service functionality:

  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymized usage data shared with Google's Firebase for analytics services.
  • Google AdMob: Device identifiers and app usage data shared for ad personalization (can be opted out).
  • No Marketing Sharing: We NEVER share personal data or identifiers with marketing companies or data brokers.
  • Legal Requirements Only: Data shared ONLY when required by law enforcement with proper legal process.
Google AdMob Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to provide free app access. AdMob collects advertising data with user consent:

  • Why AdMob?: Required revenue model for maintaining free app without subscriptions.
  • Data Used: Device identifiers, IP address, and app interaction data for ad targeting.
  • Ad Personalization: Users can opt-out of personalized ads in Google Settings > Ads.
  • Frequency Capping: Limits ad impressions to prevent user fatigue.
Data Security & Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures using Google's infrastructure:

  • Encryption: All data transmission secured with SSL/TLS encryption.
  • Google Cloud Security: Data stored in secure Google Cloud servers with ISO 27001 compliance.
  • Access Controls: Limited internal access to aggregated data only.
  • Regular Security Audits: Periodic vulnerability assessments and security updates.
Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

We are committed to children's privacy and comply with COPPA and Google Play policies:

  • No Collection Under 13: We do NOT knowingly collect any information from children under 13.
  • Age Rating: App rated for ages 3+ with no personal data collection from any age group.
  • Parental Controls: We support parental controls and family sharing features.
  • Data Minimization: All users benefit from minimal data collection approach.
Your Data Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

You have comprehensive rights over your data under privacy laws. Contact us to exercise rights:

  • Access & Portability: Right to request and receive your data in machine-readable format.
  • Deletion: Right to request complete deletion of your data from our systems.
  • Opt-Out: Opt-out of personalized advertising through Google Ads settings or app preferences.
  • No Discrimination: No penalties for exercising privacy rights.
Data Retention & Deletion

We retain data only as necessary and provide easy deletion options:

  • Analytics Data: Retained for 2 years then automatically aggregated and anonymized.
  • Crash Logs: Retained for 6 months after resolution then permanently deleted.
  • AdMob Data: Subject to Google AdMob retention policies (typically 6 months).
  • Data Deletion Requests: Processed within 30 days of verified request.
International Data Transfers

Data may be transferred globally as part of Google Cloud Services:

  • Google Cloud: Data processed and stored in Google's secure global data centers.
  • EU-US Privacy Shield: Certifications ensure adequate protection for international transfers.
  • Local Processing: Where available, data processed in EU jurisdictions for EU users.
